Who We Are

Indo-Asia Overseas Ltd. is a leading importer of Indian spices, mixes, pulses, flours and sweets in Mauritius.

We can source the best quality of food starting from basic every day foods to grains, pulses, flour, pastas, spices, sauces, jams, dressings, spreads and many more....We can source all the ready mixes and ready to eat foods in Mauritius from any Indian food product manufacturer.

Quality

At the Indo-Asia Overseas Ltd. quality assurance to our customers is a core value that we thrive to achieve at all times. This is why since 2004 we have been serving over 10,000+ clients across Mauritius. We focus on quality control across all our products to ensure that our customers get only the best.

Price

You know what is great? Top products from India at best prices. Be it festivals, birthdays, special occasions or even daily meals! Our products will be a part of your meals always. Affordable pricing makes our prodcuts the most-loved on the island.
